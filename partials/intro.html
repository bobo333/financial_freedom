How it works
Retirement, simplified

Enter in your current income, expenses, and net worth, and Plenti tells you how long until you're financially independent. It's all based on academic research used to determine how much you need to retire indefinitely.

Simulate scenarios

See the impact on your countdown to retirement if you made more or cut some monthly expenses. Plenti recommends some ways to save, earn more, and invest smartly to achieve your goal faster.

Track your progress

Watch your years to retirement decrease over time. Plenti can send reminders to tell you if you're on track according to your plan.
