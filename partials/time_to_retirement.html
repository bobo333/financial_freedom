About us
We are Steve Cipriano and Nathan Kane, the makers of Beanstalk. We believe that people lead more fulfilling lives in financial security, and when they don't need to work at jobs they dislike. We hope Beanstalk sheds light on how attainable financial freedom is for many working people. We hope it inspires the lifestyle adjustments that bring financially independence more quickly, such as smarter spending and smart investing.
