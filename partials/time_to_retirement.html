About Beanstalk

Hi there, we are Steven Cipriano and Nathan Kane.

We believe deeply that people should be empowered to spend their time how they want, and not on work they hate. We hope that Beanstalk sheds light on how attainable financial freedom is, perhaps through some lifestyle adjustments. These may include smart frugal living, like cooking and bicycling, and also smart investing, through automated, diversified stocks index funds.
